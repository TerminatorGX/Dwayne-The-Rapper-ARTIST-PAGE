Dwayne The Rapper is a Zambian Hip-Hop artist making serious noise on the global stage. Known for sharp lyricism, authentic storytelling, and unapologetic delivery, Dwayne has been carving his own lane in Zed's rap scene since the early 2020s.

With tracks like ELEMENT, BEAUTY & HORROR, SUGAR HONEY ICED TEA, and Life of a Gangster, he's shown the range — from hard-hitting street anthems to introspective cuts. Collabs with Trei Bugsey, Insane-Verbalist, Kryptiq, and Tunchi1K speak to a growing network in the scene.

2026 isn't a year — it's a takeover. ELEMENT is out now.
